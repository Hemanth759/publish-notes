## Gravity vs Others
In space big body objects like our star have tremendous pressure due to gravity to pull them inwards. Without a counter force negating this force the star might collapse into itself. Our star like many other stars use [[Nuclear Fusion|nuclear fusion]] energy to oppose this force. However when the star runs out of hydrogen gas to do nuclear fusion reaction gravity wins and star collapse into itself. Now based on the mass of the star multiple things might happen. 
1. It might become a [[White Dwarf Star]].
2. It might become a [[Neutron Star]].
3. It might become a [[Black Hole]].
